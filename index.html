About Cragens

If live music is your thing then Cragen's is the venue to visit. Chill out, have something to eat and drink, and enjoy the best live bands in Metro Manila. Cragen's is the premier live music Bar & Restaurant on Burgos.

Enjoy our wide range of cocktails, spirits, wine and local and imported beers. Our Happy Hour and monthly promo's are sure to please with our Buy 1 Take 1 specials. Our Happiest Hour promo starts from P50 on selected cocktails including Tequila Sunrise, Margaritas, Screwdrivers and Black Russians.
