About Cragens

If live music is your thing then Cragen's is the venue to visit. Chill out, have something to eat and drink, and enjoy the best live bands in Metro Manila. Cragen's is the premier live music Bar & Restaurant on Burgos.

Enjoy our wide range of cocktails, spirits, wine and local and imported beers. Our Happy Hour and monthly promo's are sure to please with our Buy 1 Take 1 specials. Our Happiest Hour promo starts from P50 on selected cocktails including Tequila Sunrise, Margaritas, Screwdrivers and Black Russians.

OUR Mission

To recognize our customer needs and continuously improve our customer experience. To serve healthy, quality and delicious food and drinks to our customers and show excellent service at a reasonable price. To help our employees strive for excellence and to continue develop their skills and knowledge. To contribute on our society by also supporting our local suppliers.

OUR Vision

To give quality service to our customers through our sumptuous and quality meals, and at the end of the day they can just enjoy the rest of the night by hanging out with their family, friends and loved ones while catching up and having the best live music they deserve after a long and tiring day. We promise to give you, not just your usual bar experience but the best bar and restaurant experience you deserve, while working also towards the greater good of our employees, community and environment at the same time.
